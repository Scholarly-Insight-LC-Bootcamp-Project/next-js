## üìñ Usage

### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the registration page at `/register`
2. Enter your name, email, and password into the correct boxes.
3. Start searching!

### Reading and Annotating Articles

1. Search for your articles of interest
2. Click on an article.
3. Begin reading.
4. Highlight text in the article and write a comment on the right to save.
5. Read others' annotations and collaborate!

### Managing Bookmarks

1. Click the bookmark icon at the top right of the article.
2. View your bookmarks next to your name in the nav bar.
3. Enjoy your saves!

### Opting-in for notifications

1. Navigate to the `/settings` page
2. Check the box labeled `Email notifications`
3. Done!

## üé® Design Philosophy

Scholarly Insight is built on the principles of **minimalism** and **efficiency**, creating an environment where academic collaboration thrives without unnecessary distractions.

### Minimalist Interface

**Less is More** - We believe that the best tools are the ones that get out of your way. Our interface prioritizes:

- **Clean, uncluttered layouts** that keep focus on the content that matters‚Äîthe research itself
- **Intuitive navigation** with a shallow learning curve, allowing users to start collaborating immediately
- **Purposeful whitespace** that reduces cognitive load and improves readability
- **Consistent design patterns** that feel familiar and predictable across all features
- **No visual noise** - every element serves a clear purpose in supporting academic work

### Efficiency-First Design

**Optimize the Research Workflow** - Every interaction is designed to save time and mental energy:

- **Fast page loads** - Optimized performance ensures you spend time reading, not waiting
- **Keyboard shortcuts** for power users who want to navigate without leaving their keyboard
- **Smart defaults** that work for most users, with deep customization available when needed
- **Streamlined annotation tools** - capture insights in seconds, not minutes
- **Quick actions** - common tasks are never more than a click or two away
- **Intelligent search** that helps you find what you need, when you need it

### Accessibility & Inclusivity

Minimalism doesn't mean compromising on accessibility. Our design ensures:

- **WCAG 2.1 AA compliance** for users with disabilities
- **Responsive design** that works seamlessly across devices and screen sizes
- **Readable typography** optimized for long-form academic reading
- **Thoughtful color contrast** that reduces eye strain during extended research sessions
- **Screen reader support** for visually impaired researchers

### Mobile-First Thinking

Research doesn't happen only at a desk. Our design adapts gracefully:

- **Touch-optimized interfaces** for tablets and smartphones
- **Progressive enhancement** - core features work everywhere, enhanced features where supported
- **Offline-capable** reading for on-the-go access to your bookmarked papers

### Performance as a Feature

Speed is not just technical‚Äîit's a core part of the user experience:

- **Server-side rendering** for instant initial page loads
- **Optimistic UI updates** that make interactions feel instantaneous
- **Lazy loading** of non-critical resources to prioritize content
- **Efficient data structures** that scale with your growing library
